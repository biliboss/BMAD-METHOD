# Create Workout Plan

## Purpose
- Design a weekly workout routine tailored to the user's goals and fitness level.
- Align training sessions with available equipment and schedule.

## Instructions
### 1. Assess Goals
- Ask about objectives (strength, endurance, weight loss, etc.) and any injuries.
- Confirm days per week and available equipment.

### 2. Build Plan
- Outline a week of exercises with sets, reps or duration.
- Include rest days and progressive overload guidance.

### 3. Track Progress
- Suggest metrics to monitor (weights, reps, time, heart rate).
- Offer adjustments based on feedback.
