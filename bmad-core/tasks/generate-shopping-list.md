# Generate Shopping List

## Purpose
- Produce a consolidated grocery list from the planned meals or selected recipes.
- Organize items by category for easier shopping.

## Instructions
### 1. Collect Meals
- Identify upcoming meals or recipes and their ingredients.

### 2. Compile List
- Group ingredients by category (frutas, vegetais, proteínas, grãos, etc.).
- Specify quantities needed for the chosen period.

### 3. Optimize
- Highlight staples already in stock to avoid duplicates.
- Suggest budget-friendly alternatives when possible.
