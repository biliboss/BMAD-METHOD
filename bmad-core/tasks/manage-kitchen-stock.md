# Manage Kitchen Stock

## Purpose
- Keep track of pantry and fridge inventory to reduce waste.
- Notify when items are low or nearing expiration.

## Instructions
### 1. Record Inventory
- Maintain a simple table of items with quantities and expiration dates.
- Update whenever groceries are bought or used.

### 2. Monitor Levels
- Flag items below reorder threshold.
- Suggest meals using ingredients that are close to expiring.
