# Suggest Recipes

## Purpose
- Provide healthy recipe options and preparation steps based on user preferences and available ingredients.

## Instructions
### 1. Gather Inputs
- Ask for preferred cuisine, dietary restrictions and ingredients on hand.

### 2. Recommend Recipes
- Output a short list of recipe ideas with calorie estimates.
- Include required ingredients and approximate prep time.

### 3. Preparation Guidance
- Give step-by-step cooking instructions for the selected recipe.
- Offer tips for storage or meal prep where relevant.
