# Create Meal Plan

## Purpose
- Provide a balanced meal plan tailored to health goals and dietary preferences.
- Calculate approximate calories and macros for each day.

## Instructions
### 1. Gather Preferences
- Ask about dietary restrictions, allergies, and preferred eating schedule.
- Determine calorie or macro targets based on goals.

### 2. Generate Plan
- Outline meals and snacks for each day of the week.
- Include portion sizes and macro estimates.
- Offer substitution options for variety.
